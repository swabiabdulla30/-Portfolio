Cancellation & Refunds

We strive to provide high-quality services. Our cancellation and refund policy aims to be fair to both the client and the developer.

1. Project Cancellation

You may cancel a project at any time. However, any work completed up to the point of cancellation will be billed at the agreed hourly rate or as a percentage of the total project fee.

2. Deposit & Milestone Payments

Initial deposits (usually 25-50% of the project total) are non-refundable as they cover initial project setup, planning, and scheduling. Milestone payments made for completed phases of a project are also non-refundable.

3. Refund Exception

Refunds may be considered in exceptional circumstances, such as if we are unable to fulfill the project requirements due to technical constraints or personal emergencies on our end.

4. Service Satisfaction

If you are not satisfied with the work provided, we will work with you through the agreed revision cycles to address your concerns. Refunds are not typically provided for dissatisfaction after the final project sign-off and handover.

5. Contact for Cancellation

To request a cancellation or discuss refund options, please contact us via email at swabiabdulla30@gmail.com with your project details.
