Shipping Policy

Since Sabiyy provides digital services (Web Design & Development), the following policies apply to the delivery of these services.

1. Digital Delivery

All project deliverables, including source code, design assets, and documentation, are delivered digitally via secure cloud storage links, Git repositories, or email. No physical shipping is involved.

2. Delivery Timeline

The estimated delivery timeline for each project is agreed upon during the initial consultation and detailed in the project proposal. Any delays due to feedback cycles or scope changes will be communicated promptly.

3. Revision Cycles

Most projects include a specific number of revision cycles (usually 2-3) to ensure the final product meets your expectations. Revisions beyond the agreed scope may incur additional charges and affect delivery timelines.

4. International Clients

We work with clients worldwide. Digital delivery ensures that geography does not affect the speed or quality of service delivery.

5. Technical Support

Post-delivery support (bug fixes or minor adjustments) is usually provided for a period of 14 days after final project handover, unless otherwise specified in a maintenance agreement.
